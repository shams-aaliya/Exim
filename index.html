About Us

Exim Enterprises was founded by the Proprietor Mohammed Shamse Azam in the year 2000. Since then, we have been manufacturing haversack bags, trolley bags, laptop bags, leather products, corporate gift set,etc. We manufacture products in bulk and hence there is a minimum order policy of atleast 100 products in any category. We follow a very detailed process to create every piece of our product or sample. From cutting the material, stitching, strapping all the components together we check them each for quality assurance before  packaging and then we deliver it to our clients with great satisfaction on both sides. We also manufacture t-shirts, caps, multi utility pouches, umbrella, uniforms with company logo printed on them i.e completely customized as per our clients wishes. Our process is very clear and precise where the client gets us the sample and after studying the structure and material used thoroughly we quote a price which is reasonable. We have been often referred as "Bag manufacturers", "Trolley bag manufacturers", "Laptop bag manufacturers" but we manufacture all kinds of promotional gift items there is. Companies like Varroc Engineering, Eureka Forbes, Birla, Itvara travels, Zenith hospitality continue to be our clients after many years of customer-relationship experience. And we only look forward to increase our clientele. We not only deliver products in the country but we have gone international. Contact us from any corner of the world and we will deliver you the hand manufactured product at your door.

Our Mission

We are on a mission to provide our customers the best quality products at utmost reasonable pricing. And we input all our hardwork into manufacturing that curated product for you with utmost love.

Our Values

The core values of our company is integrity, hardwork and loyalty. We in our company work as a team to manufacture your corporate gift material and deliver it to you on time. We are loyal to our customers hence we take customer feedback very seriously.

Our Vision

We believe in our team of kaarigar ! Our craftsmen  come from the rural areas of Bihar and we believe in their craftsmenship and uniqueness. Our vision is to provide these craftsmen a platform to present their craft.
